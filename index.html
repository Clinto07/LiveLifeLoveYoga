Why Practice Yoga With Us?

Feel Healthy

Since it increases flexibility creates increased muscle strength and tone. Improves respiration energy and vitality. Helps maintaining a balanced metabolism. Helps with weight reduction cardio and circulatory health. Gives improved athletic performance and protection from injury.

Mindfulness

Helps gain deep sense of awareness / opening into the self. Helps one to face the challenges of everyday life / demonstrate less reactivity. Gives a heightened sense of compassion and non-judgment for the self and others. Helps deepen your personal yoga practice. Relieves anxiety & stress.
